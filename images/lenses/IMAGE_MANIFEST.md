## Use rule

The selected Learning Emphasis changes the week-opening visual world, the in-week spotlight, and the walkthrough cover and spotlight. The image is part of the teaching route, not decoration.

- Western social science foregrounds disciplinary institutions, conventional methods, archives, categories, and historical conditions.
- Two-Eyed Seeing foregrounds accountable relationship between distinct knowledge systems. It does not blend them into a generic diversity frame.
- Indigenous scholarship stays attached to the named Nation, people, community, place, project, or course source. It is not a pan-Indigenous holistic category.
- No lone portrait represents a week, culture, country, Nation, people, discipline, or emphasis.
- Named scholars, Elders, Survivors, and knowledge holders remain in the in-week content where the course can provide context and attribution.
- A real photograph does not prove the teaching claim. Captions state the visual's limited teaching job.

The exact student-facing statement, caption, alt text, credit, source URL, crop position, and gallery structure are canonical in `data/soc122-hero-theme.js`. The existing named-person and named-community spotlight anchors remain canonical in `data/soc122-cases.js` and `data/soc122-images.js`.

## Accessibility and representation gate

- All current records resolve to local files and load successfully.
- The smallest new source-derived image is 763 pixels wide after responsive preparation; the Bras d'Or image was replaced with the 2000-pixel original.
- Every state has a visible route label, explanatory statement, caption, credit, meaningful alt text, and source link unless the image is explicitly instructor-created.
- Week 3 and Week 8 use three-image galleries because one image cannot carry the required plurality.
- Archival maps and the globe use `contain` treatment so magnification and narrow crops do not remove the essential visual relationship.
- Colour is supplementary. Route text, border shape, image content, primary writing, and source order carry the distinction.
